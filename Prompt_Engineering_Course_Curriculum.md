# Complete Prompt Engineering Course Curriculum
## Table of Contents

### **Module 1: Foundations of Prompt Engineering**
#### 1.1 Introduction to AI and Large Language Models
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Topics**:
  - Evolution of AI and NLP
  - Understanding LLMs (GPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
  - How LLMs work: Transformers, attention mechanisms
  - Tokenization and context windows
- **Hands-on**: Interactive exploration of different LLM interfaces
- **Practical**: Building your first prompt and understanding responses

#### 1.2 What is Prompt Engineering?
- **Duration**: 1.5 hours
- **Topics**:
  - Definition and importance of prompt engineering
  - Role of prompt engineers in industry
  - Market demand and salary expectations
  - Career paths and opportunities
- **Case Study**: Real-world prompt engineering success stories
- **Practical**: Analyzing job postings and requirements

#### 1.3 Understanding AI Model Capabilities and Limitations
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Topics**:
  - Model strengths and weaknesses
  - Hallucinations and how to minimize them
  - Bias in AI models
  - Ethical considerations in prompt engineering
- **Hands-on**: Testing model limitations with various prompts
- **Practical**: Creating prompts that work across different models

Upon completion, students will be able to:
1. Design and implement effective prompts for various use cases
2. Build production-ready AI applications
3. Implement RAG systems and advanced AI architectures
4. Optimize AI systems for performance and cost
5. Apply prompt engineering in real-world business scenarios
6. Pursue careers as Prompt Engineers, AI Engineers, or AI Product Managers
